ABOUT OUR CEMENTS

Cement is an important material in construction.Sheets,blocks,pillers and pipes are made from concrete from cement.
Cement is a dry,greenih grey powder with fine particles.
It Is Made From silica(sand),Alumina(aluminium oxide),Lime,Iron oxide and Magnesia(magnesium oxide)
WE HAVE TWO TYPES OF CEMENT
TYPE-1
Type one is a MODERATE SULFATE RESISTANT Cement.
important when concrete i cast against soil That has moderate sulfate levels,but irrelevant to concrete countertops.
TYPE-2
Type two is a HIGH EARLY STRENGTH Cement.
It ground finer and reacts faster than type 2.
So the early compressive strength gains are greater.
